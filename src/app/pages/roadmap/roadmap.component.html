OZARUS Roadmap

Q2 2025 – Launch & Early Adoption (April-August 2025)

  • Branding and launch of OZARUS website (V1)
  • Smart contract scanner with paste-to-analyze feature
  • Token smart contract audit
  • LLaunch of the OZARUS Token in July-August 2025
  • DEX listing and liquidity locking
  • Token-based analytics dashboard
  • Risk scoring algorithm (v1.5)

Q4 2025 – Ecosystem Expansion (October – December 2025)

  • Multi-chain scanner support (Ethereum, BSC, Base)
  • Governance system for token holders
  • CEX listing strategy
  • Open API for developers and integrations

Q3 2025 – Utility & Growth (July – September 2025)

  • Release of the OZARUS browser extension (Chrome/Brave)
  • Integration with token launch platforms (e.g., Pump.fun)
  • Unlockable features via OZARUS Token
  • Community leaderboard and rewards system
  • First security/audit partnerships

Q1 2026 – Scaling Up (January – March 2026)

  • Launch of advanced AI-based risk engine (v2.0)
  • Real-time scam detection alerts
  • Integration with wallets and token creation tools
  • Full SDK and documentation for devs and partners
